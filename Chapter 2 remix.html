Chapter 2 Remix: A Modern Twist

Introduction

Let's dive into the details of Fortnite's Chapter 2 Remix, released in November 2024. This updated chapter brings a modern twist to the game, featuring celebrity collabs, revamped locations, and exciting new weapons

Weapons

Assault rifle


Assault Weapons are typically an all-round class of weapons that are used for medium ranged combat and quickly destroying structures. Assault Weapons often have first shot accuracy, meaning they can fire their first bullet without any bloom.

Burst Assault rifle


The Burst Assault Rifle is a burst-fire, medium ranged weapon that fires 2-round burst shots (during Fortnite OG, the weapon fired in a 3-round burst). It excels at medium ranged combat, but has the ability to perform decently at long ranges.

Drum gun


Drum Gun is a fully automatic, fast firing, closer ranged weapon dealing high amounts of damage in a close quarters environment, but being much weaker at extended ranges due to poor accuracy.

Pump shotgun


The Pump Shotgun is a high damage, short range and slow firing shotgun, best suited to very close quarters. It has 5 shells in the magazine and must be pumped between shots, leading a slow fire rate.

Tactical Shotgun


The Tactical Shotgun is a fully automatic, short ranged weapon. It fires a string of pellets when shot, which tighten on aiming down sights. The weapon can be fired continuously with no effect on accuracy.

Heavy Sniper


The Heavy Sniper Rifle is a Fast-Moving Projectile weapon. As it uses a scope, the fired bullet will always shoot from the center of the crosshair. There is no damage falloff on the Heavy Sniper Rifle's projectiles.

Weapons Vaulted:


Dual Micro SMGs - A pair of submachine guns that allowed for rapid dual-wielding fire. Vaulted to refine SMG gameplay mechanics.

Striker Burst Rifle - A burst-fire rifle known for its high damage output and accuracy. Vaulted to make room for new weapon dynamics.

Sovereign Shotgun - A powerful shotgun with a wide spread, ideal for close-quarters combat. Vaulted to balance shotgun options in the game.

Monarch Pistol - A powerful sidearm known for its high damage output and accuracy. Vaulted to make room for new weapon dynamics.

Hyper SMG - Hyper SMG is a medium damage, high fire-rate submachine gun with a short range. It is equipped with a Red Eye Sight.

Thunder Burst SMG - Thunder Burst SMG is a high damage submachine gun that fires in a three round burst configuration. It is equipped with a Holo-13 Optic and Suppressor.

Nemesis AR - Nemesis AR is a medium damage, high fire rate assault rifle with 25 shots per magazine, equipped with a Holo-13 Optic.

Striker AR - Striker AR is a medium damage, high firerate assault rifle equipped with a Red Eye Sight and a Suppressor.

Gatekeeper Shotgun - Gatekeeper Shotgun is a fast firing, close range shotgun with a greater effective range than other similar weapons. The Gatekeeper Shotgun uses Shells and has a headshot multiplier of 1.5x, and a max damage cap of 150.

Hammer Pump Shotgun - Hammer Pump Shotgun is a high damage, slow firing pump shotgun, that uses shells and has headshot multiplier of 3x and a max damage cap of 180.

Frenzy Auto Shotgun - Frenzy Auto Shotgun is an automatic shotgun with a fast fire rate. Unlike most Shotguns, the Frenzy Auto Shotgun reloads all shells at once, instead of one at a time. It is equipped with a Red Eye Sight.

Reaper Sniper Rifle - Reaper Sniper Rifle is a high damage sniper rifle with 3 shots per magazine, that uses Heavy Bullets and has a headshot multiplier of 5x.
