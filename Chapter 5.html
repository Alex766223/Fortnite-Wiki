Chapter 5: Evolution Continues

Introduction

This chapter explores the ongoing evolution of the game, introducing new mechanics, challenges, and updates designed to enhance the player experience. With the additon of a new object called a Medallion doing something different in each season of Chapter 5.

Weapons

Weapons Added:


Assault Rifles


Nemesis AR (Season 1)


Nemesis AR is a medium damage, high fire rate assault rifle with 25 shots per magazine, equipped with a Holo-13 Optic. Has a Mythic varient that can be dropped by killing Montague at Grand Glacier

Striker AR (Season 1)


Striker AR is a medium damage, high firerate assault rifle equipped with a Red Eye Sight and a Suppressor. Has a Mythic Varient that can be dropped by killing the boss Nisha at Fencing Fields

Enforcer AR (Season 1)


Enforcer AR is a fully automatic, high damage assault rifle with a slow fire rate equipped with a P2X Optic.

Warforged AR (Season 2)


The Modular Warforged Assault Rifle is an automatic, medium-range weapon that fires moderately fast and excels at medium and close ranged combat, but suffers at longer ranges due to its high recoil.

Huntress DMR (Season 2)


Modular Huntress DMR is a fully automatic, high damage marksman rifle with a very slow fire rate, that uses Medium Bullets - Ammunition - Fortnite Medium Bullets and has a headshot multiplier of 1.75x

Shotguns


Hammer Pump Shotgun (Season 1)


Hammer Pump Shotgun is a high damage, slow firing pump shotgun and has a headshot multiplier of 3x and a max damage cap of 180. Has a mythic varient by killing the boss Peter Griffin at Snoopy Steps

Frenzy Auto Shotgun (Season 1)


Frenzy Auto Shotgun is an automatic shotgun with a fast fire rate. Unlike most Shotguns, the Frenzy Auto Shotgun reloads all shells at once, instead of one at a time. It is equipped with a Red Eye Sight. Has a mythic Varient by killing the boss Oscar at Lavish lair

Gatekeeper Shotgun (Season 2)


The Modular Gatekeeper Shotgun is a fast firing, close range shotgun with a greater effective range than other similar weapons. The Gatekeeper Shotgun uses Shells - Ammunition - Fortnite Shells, has a headshot multiplier of 1.5x, and a max damage cap of 150.

SMG's


Hyper SMG (Season 1)


Hyper SMG is a medium damage, high fire-rate submachine gun with a short range. It is equipped with a Red Eye Sight.

Thunder Burst SMG (Season 1)


Thunder Burst SMG is a high damage submachine gun that fires in a three round burst configuration. It is equipped with a Holo-13 Optic and Suppressor.

Snipers


Reaper Sniper Rifle (Season 1)


Reaper Sniper Rifle is a high damage sniper rifle with 3 shots per magazine, that uses Heavy Bullets and has a headshot multiplier of 5x.

Explosives


Anvil Rocket Launcher (Season 1)


The Anvil Rocket Launcher will lock onto vehicles if the player holds the crosshair of the weapon on one for roughly 1.75 seconds when aiming with it. After this, the player can launch a rocket which moves vertically, and falls straight down onto the vehicle.

Cluster Clingers (Season 1)


The Cluster Clinger is a throwable explosive. Like other throwables, holding the aim button outlines a projectile trail and pressing fire will throw it. When the Cluster Clinger is thrown, it will stick to the first entity or solid surface that it makes contact with (this can include players).

Shield Breaker EMP (Season 1)


Shield Breaker EMPs are EMP grenades that disable electronic devices such as turrets, cars or even shield bubbles for 10 seconds. They can also be thrown at Vending Machines in order to cause them to malfunction and drop 50-150 Bars.

Weapons Vaulted:



Shockwave Hammer (Chapter 4 Season 1) - The Shockwave Hammer has 2 functions. Pressing the fire button will cause the character to slam the hammer on the ground, sending a shockwave that damages nearby structures and opponents, launching them away.

Flapjack Rifle (Chapter 4 Season 3) - The Flapjack Rifle is a full-auto Assault Weapon that features a large 45-round magazine and fast fire rate. It is suitable for use at close and medium range, but the recoil must be controlled for use at longer distances.

Supressed Pistol (Chapter 4 Season 4) - The Suppressed Pistol is a fully automatic, high fire-rate cap, any range weapon, but it excels at close to medium range combat. The weapon is mounted with a suppressor meaning it does not appear on an enemy compass when fired, and is not audible at longer ranges.

Mammoth Pistol (Chapter 4 Season 3) - The Mammoth Pistol is a medium to long range, single-shot pistol that works similarly to the Hand Cannon, but with higher damage and only one shot before needing to reload.

Bosses


Oscar (Season 1)



Located at Lavish lair, Oscar is one of the bosses introduced in Season 1. Defeating Oscar grants players access to his Mythic weapon and the vault he guards.

Nisha (Season 1)



Another boss from Season 1, Nisha can be found in Fencing Fields. Overcoming Nisha provides players with her exclusive Mythic weapon and access to valuable loot.

Montague (Season 1)



Montague is stationed at his own lair at Grand Glacier. Players who defeat Montague receive his Mythic weapon and can access the vault he protects.

Valeria (Season 1)



As the leader of The Society, Valeria is a formidable boss in Season 1. Confronting and defeating her offers players unique rewards, including her Mythic weapon.

Zeus (Season 2)



In the Greek mythology-themed Season 2, Zeus is a prominent boss. Players can find him at Mount Olympus. Defeating Zeus rewards players with his Mythic weapon and other exclusive items.

Ares (Season 2)



In the Greek mythology-themed Season 2, Zeus is a prominent boss. Players can find him at Mount Olympus. Defeating Zeus rewards players with his Mythic weapon and other exclusive items.

Cerberus (Season 2)



As a challenging boss in Season 2, Cerberus guards his lair. Defeating Cerberus grants players his Mythic weapon and access to the vault he protects.

Hades (Season 2)



As a challenging boss in Season 2, Cerberus guards his lair. Defeating Cerberus grants players his Mythic weapon and access to the vault he protects.
