OG Fortnite: A Blast From The Past

Introduction

Fortnite has evolved tremendously since its early days, but for many players, the original version holds a special place in their hearts. With iconic weapons, map changes, and unforgettable moments, let’s take a trip down memory lane and revisit what made OG Fortnite so memorable.

Weapons

Weapons Added:


Pump Shotgun


A fan-favorite, the Pump Shotgun became the weapon of choice for close-range combat. Its ability to deal high damage in one shot made it a staple in any player's arsenal.

Scar (Assault Rifle)

The Legendary and Epic versions of the Scar were top-tier in terms of damage output and accuracy.

Rocket Launcher


The Rocket Launcher offered devastating explosive power, perfect for taking down structures or multiple enemies at once.

Weapons Vaulted:


Dual Pistols


Though not the most popular, the Dual Pistols offered a unique dual-wielding experience that was vaulted after the early days of the game.

Crossbow

While it provided an interesting mechanic with its ability to fire bolts, it didn’t quite make a lasting impact and was eventually removed.

Tactical Submachine Gun

A favorite for many due to its fast rate of fire, the Tactical SMG was vaulted to make room for new weapons and meta shifts.
