Chapter 4: The Next Generation

Introduction

Fortnite: Chapter 4 introduced a new map, fresh mechanics, and innovative features like the new Unreal Engine 5 enhancements. The chapter also brought new collaborations and major updates to keep gameplay exciting for both new and returning players.

Weapons

Weapons Added:


Ex-Caliber Rifle


A new weapon that delivers explosive projectiles with each shot, adding a tactical new option for players.

Thunder Shotgun


A pump-action shotgun that offers significant damage up close, with a strong focus on high-impact shots.

Twin Mag SMG

A submachine gun that features a double magazine, allowing for quicker reloads and sustained fire.

Maven Auto Shotgun


A new automatic shotgun that provides rapid, close-range fire and is perfect for fast-paced combat.

Weapons Vaulted:


Primal Shotgun


Vaulted as part of the seasonal transition and the shift towards newer weaponry.

Makeshift Weapons


These have been vaulted in favor of more modern firearms and balance changes.

Bosses


The Ageless Champion


A formidable new boss who is part of the storyline and is located at a key location on the map. Players can defeat him for unique rewards.

Kader


A new NPC/character added to Chapter 4 with special abilities. Boss encounters with Kader are designed to be challenging, offering great loot.

The Herald (Return)


The Herald might have a continued presence in Chapter 4, with new forms and interactions, continuing her storyline from Chapter 3.
