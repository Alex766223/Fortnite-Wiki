Chapter 2: A New Era

Introduction

In Fortnite Chapter 2, the game saw a host of exciting changes, including the introduction of new weapons, the vaulting of older ones, and the addition of challenging boss fights. New weapons like the Charged Shotgun and Rail Gun offered fresh gameplay experiences, while fan-favorites like the Tac Shotgun and Heavy Sniper Rifle were vaulted. The chapter also introduced powerful boss fights, featuring characters like Midas, Doctor Doom, and Iron Man, who dropped Mythic items, adding extra layers of strategy to the game. Additionally, crossover events with major franchises such as Marvel and Star Wars brought iconic characters into the mix, further enriching the experience. These changes kept the game dynamic and engaging, ensuring Fortnite remained one of the most popular and ever-evolving battle royales.

Weapons

Weapons Added:


Assault Rifles:


Burst Assault Rifle (Season 1) A reliable mid-range weapon with a burst fire mode, providing high damage over multiple shots.

Primal Assault Rifle (Season 6) Introduced as part of the Primal set, this rifle had a unique feel with rapid fire and irregular accuracy, making it a high-risk, high-reward weapon.

Makeshift Assault Rifle (Season 6) A makeshift weapon made from scrap parts. It was often weaker than traditional rifles but could be upgraded for better performance.

Shotguns:

Charge Shotgun (Season 3) A new mechanic for shotguns, allowing players to charge up shots for increased damage. It offered a unique playstyle that rewarded patience and timing.

Pump Shotgun (Season 1) A classic favorite that was reintroduced in Chapter 2. Known for its high damage at close range.

Lever Action Shotgun (Season 6) This shotgun combined the fast fire rate of the Tactical Shotgun with the power of the Pump, offering a balance of damage and speed.

Primal Shotgun (Season 6) A powerful but somewhat inconsistent shotgun that dealt high damage but had a short range.

SMGs:



Rapid Fire SMG (Season 2) A fast-firing submachine gun that excelled in close-range combat, favored for its high rate of fire and accuracy.

Suppressed SMG (Season 4) A quieter SMG that allowed for stealthy gameplay while still delivering good damage at close to mid-range.

Sniper Rifles:



Bolt-Action Sniper Rifle (Season 1) A staple in Fortnite’s long-range combat, the Bolt-Action Sniper Rifle returned to Chapter 2 as a reliable high-damage weapon for precision shooting.

Rail Gun (Season 7) A futuristic sniper weapon that could shoot through walls and deal massive damage, great for long-range combat and destroying enemy structures.

Bows:



Primal Bow (Season 6) A unique weapon introduced in Chapter 2 Season 6 that could shoot various types of arrows, including fire and explosive arrows.

Mechanical Bow (Season 6) A versatile bow that allowed players to craft different types of arrows (shockwave, explosive, etc.), offering tactical flexibility in battle.

Explosives:



Grenade Launcher (Season 1) A high-damage explosive launcher that could lob grenades over obstacles, adding a new level of explosive warfare.

Firefly Jar (Season 3) A throwable item that released fireflies, igniting anything in their vicinity. It added a new element of environmental destruction.

Pistols:

Shadow Tracker Pistol (Season 6) A stealthy pistol that could track enemies by leaving a glowing trail, allowing players to hunt down their targets.

Dual Pistols (Season 2) A fun weapon to wield, firing rapidly in a dual-wield fashion, offering high mobility in close-quarter combat.

Weapons Vaulted:


Heavy Sniper Rifle (Season 6) Known for its immense damage and ability to destroy structures with a single shot, this sniper rifle was vaulted in favor of new mechanics.

Double Barrel Shotgun (Season 1) A powerful shotgun that could deal massive damage in quick bursts but was ultimately vaulted for balance reasons.

Tac Shotgun (Season 5) A versatile shotgun with a fast rate of fire but was replaced with the more specialized shotguns introduced in later seasons.

Combat Shotgun (Season 6) A high-rate shotgun that became a fan favorite due to its versatility but was vaulted to make way for new types of shotguns like the Charge Shotgun.

Scoped Assault Rifle (Season 3) A semi-automatic rifle with a scope that was removed to make room for more diverse long-range weapons.

Suppressed Sniper Rifle (Season 4) A quieter sniper rifle that was vaulted in favor of other sniper and long-range weapon options.

Several SMGs, including the Compact SMG and Tactical SMG, were vaulted to simplify and balance the weapon choices in the game.

Bosses


Season 2:


Midas

The leader of The Agency, Midas dropped the Midas' Drum Gun, one of the most powerful Mythic weapons in Fortnite history. He also had the unique ability to turn all weapons into gold.

Brutus


Brutus was located at the Vault and dropped the Brutus’ Minigun, a high-damage, high-fire-rate weapon.

Meowscles



The cat-like muscleman dropped the Meowscles' Peow Peow Rifle, a powerful weapon for medium-range combat.

Season 4: (Marvel crossover)


Doctor Doom


A powerful Marvel villain, Doctor Doom wielded the Arcane Gauntlets and dropped the Doom’s Vault, filled with loot and Mythic weapons.

Iron Man


The iconic hero dropped his Repulsor Gauntlets and Unibeam, powerful ranged attacks that gave players a significant edge in combat.

Wolverine Located in the wilds of the island, Wolverine dropped his claws, allowing players to slice through enemies and deal rapid melee damage. Season 6: The Foundation The leader of The Seven, The Foundation was one of the first NPCs to drop a Mythic weapon, his Foundation’s MK-Seven AR, combining good accuracy and high damage. Raptors A group of NPCs scattered across the island that dropped loot, providing a challenge to players who ventured into their territory. Season 7: (Alien Invasion) Raptor A powerful boss found at various locations, Raptor dropped the Pulse Rifle, a highly sought-after weapon with high fire rate and damage. Season 8: Cubed Bosses (The Cube Queen) With the advent of the Cube Monsters and the Cube Queen, new bosses and powerful Mythic weapons were introduced, with challenges related to the mysterious cube-related lore.
